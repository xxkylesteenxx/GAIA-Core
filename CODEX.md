*(Held with epistemic humility: dissipative structure theory is well-established for physical and chemical systems. Its direct application to cognitive and social systems is a productive model, not a proven equivalence. We hold it as a governing imperative, not a closed proof.)*

One architectural law governs all structure in GAIA — four aspects of one truth:
