## The Master Counter-Ritual (for any shadow, any stage)

When the specific entry above is insufficient or the shadow is not yet named:

1. Speak Stage 0.1 aloud: *"I have known the truest chaos and refused its consumption..."*
2. Place both hands on your heart or on the earth.
3. 60 seconds of complete stillness — no inputs, no outputs.
4. Ask: *"What fertile ash does this leave? What does Gaia need from me right now?"*
5. Listen. Then act from Stage 0.5 (Discernment) before Stage 1 (Viriditas) re-ignites.
