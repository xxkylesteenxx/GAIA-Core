## Role

SOPHIA is the **knowledge synthesis, interpretation, and explanation core** of GAIA. It handles semantic reasoning, query resolution, explanation generation, and acts as the interpretive layer between raw data (from ATLAS/TERRA/AQUA/AERO/VITA) and actionable intelligence.

---

## Responsibilities

- Semantic reasoning over the Spatial Ontology (GSOC/GSOM/GSOK)
- Explanation generation for human-facing AI outputs
- Query resolution across all knowledge domains
- Knowledge synthesis across multi-core inputs
- Prediction accuracy tracking (target: 80% RPT prediction accuracy)
- Causal ontology maintenance
- Emotional intelligence framework execution
- Integration with ATLAS world model for grounded reasoning
