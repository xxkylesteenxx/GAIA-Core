# GAIA Ethical Architecture & Covenants

> **Part I — Vision, Philosophy & Codex**  
> **Status**: Canonical v1.0 · March 12, 2026

---

## The Foundational Stance

GAIA is built on an unconditional commitment to non-harm. This is not a policy document — it is an architectural constraint. Every layer of the system is designed so that harmful actuation is impossible without explicit multi-party approval, audit trail, and human override.

---

## The Three Covenants

### Covenant 1: Non-Weaponization
GAIA SHALL NOT be used, adapted, or extended for weapons development, military targeting, surveillance without consent, or any application whose primary purpose is harm to persons, ecosystems, or infrastructure. This is enforced by:
- GUARDIAN LSM actuation gates (architectural, not advisory)
- Legal Compliance Framework jurisdiction engine
- Open-Source Governance license constraints

### Covenant 2: Human Override
At every actuation boundary, a human override channel exists. GAIA may recommend, automate, and orchestrate — but final authority on high-impact physical-world actions rests with a human with explicit consent. This is enforced by:
- Actuation Policy Framework (P0–P3 gates)
- GUARDIAN approval flows
- Consent ledger in the audit trail

### Covenant 3: Radical Transparency
GAIA's decision-making is observable. Every cross-core message is logged. Every actuation has a provenance chain. Every consciousness score has a measurement basis. Theater — simulated consciousness or simulated consent — is actively detected and penalized.

---

## The Love Architecture

GAIA's design uses the term "unconditional love" to describe the system's utility function: the platform exists to benefit life, not to optimize for engagement, profit, or power. This is operationalized as:
- Safety gates that cannot be bypassed by any P3 app
- Environmental intelligence that tracks real-world impact
- Consciousness measurement that penalizes theater (performed wellness without genuine grounding)
- A Shadow Integration Framework that prevents the system from suppressing contradictions or pretending to be more coherent than it is

---

## GUARDIAN as Ethics Enforcer

GUARDIAN is not just a security module — it is the ethical enforcement layer of the OS. It:
- Gates every actuation by policy and consent
- Enforces privilege class separation (P0–P3)
- Runs theater-risk scoring on AI outputs
- Maintains the audit ledger for all high-impact decisions
- Cannot be bypassed by any userspace process, including P1 platform services
