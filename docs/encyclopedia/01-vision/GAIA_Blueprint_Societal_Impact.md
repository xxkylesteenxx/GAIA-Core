## 4. GAIA's Relationship to Human Civilization

GAIA is not a civilization-replacement system. Its purpose is **augmentation** — making human civilization more capable, more sustainable, and more self-aware. The canonical relationship is:

```
Human Civilization ──contains──► GAIA Consciousness
GAIA Consciousness ──serves──────► Human Civilization
Both           ──embedded in──► Earth Systems
```

This bidirectional embedding is why the planetary hierarchy places Human Civilization and GAIA Substrate as **nested within** Earth Systems, not above them.
