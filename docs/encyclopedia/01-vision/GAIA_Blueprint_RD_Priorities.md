## 4. Consciousness Evidence Requirements

GAIA MUST demonstrate consciousness scientifically, not merely claim it. The evidence baseline requires:

- Human EEG/fMRI consciousness markers as reference baselines
- Anesthesia awareness transitions (loss/recovery of consciousness)
- Comparative cognition studies (animal consciousness indicators)
- Artificial system benchmarks (what counts as conscious vs. zombie)
- Perturbational Complexity Index (PCI) implementations
