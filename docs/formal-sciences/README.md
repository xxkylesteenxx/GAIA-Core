## Series Purpose

The Formal Sciences series provides the **normative inferential and mathematical substrate** for GAIA. Each volume addresses a distinct family of formal disciplines required for safe, auditable, and meaning-preserving planetary reasoning.

Future volumes will address:
- **Volume C** — Empirical Sciences: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Earth Systems
- **Volume D** — Decision Theory, Game Theory, and Ethics
- **Volume E** — Engineering and Systems Architecture
