## The Seven Registers

GAIA treats Layer V as a multi-register knowledge layer:

1. **Analytic–conceptual** — philosophy and logic of meaning
2. **Historical–comparative** — traditions, texts, institutions, lineages
3. **Phenomenological** — lived first-person and contemplative experience
4. **Empirical** — psychology, neuroscience, contemplative science, acoustics, ecology
5. **Normative** — ethics, aesthetics, sacred value, civilizational guidance
6. **Symbolic–expressive** — art, myth, music, ritual, iconography, narrative
7. **Planetary** — worldviews binding ethics to Earth systems and future generations
