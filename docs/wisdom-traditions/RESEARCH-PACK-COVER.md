The pack follows the same canonical pattern as the Formal Sciences, Natural Sciences, Human Sciences, and Applied Sciences & Engineering packs:
- Master report for boundary conditions and architecture
- Domain volumes for depth
- Explicit separation of empirical, interpretive, doctrinal, phenomenological, and speculative claims
- GAIA-specific implementation implications throughout
